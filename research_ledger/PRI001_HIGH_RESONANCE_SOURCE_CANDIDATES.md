Candidate value:

```text
BIC and topological-BIC systems directly address long-lived resonant modes, high-Q confinement, boundary/edge/corner localization, and mode tunability in open photonic structures.
```

Relevant gates:

```text
PG1 native carrier: partial
PG3 relay/transport: partial
PG4 persistent support identity: candidate
PG5 intrinsic readout: open
PG7 harnessability: partial
PG8 negative control: required
```

```text
HR-SRC-002 Kodigala et al., Lasing action from photonic bound states in continuum, Nature 2017, DOI 10.1038/nature20799
```

Kill notes:

