## 1. Purpose

The repeated residual intuition is:

```text
formation replaces computation
```

This phrase is too easy to misuse unless the repo states what fails.

This artifact defines candidate negative controls: systems or descriptions that may compute, simulate, visualize, or optimize something related to positive geometry but still fail the Formation Medium test.

## 2. Negative Control Principle

A candidate fails Formation Medium if its apparent structure depends primarily on:

```text
external encoding
externally programmed update rules
user-designated output registers
simulation of target geometry
ordinary optimization objective
mere visualization of boundary modes
transport/routing without intrinsic readout
```

Failure does not mean the system is useless. It may still be useful as:

```text
simulation tool
benchmark
prototype support layer
visualization device
negative control
validator generator
engineering component
```

But it should not be called a Formation Medium.
