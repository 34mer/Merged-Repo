# Independent Witness Verification

## Witness target

Ask 3-10 independent technical witnesses to verify only the narrow technical claim.

## Claim to verify

We verified lossless migration of a complete digital worm-like organism process between computational substrates.

## Witness confirmation line

`I reviewed the repo and reproduced the migration verification.`

## What witnesses should verify

- The repository contains the deterministic digital worm migration protocol.
- The process is checkpointed.
- The checkpoint is copied or transferred.
- The copied checkpoint hash matches the source checkpoint hash.
- The process resumes from the copied checkpoint.
- The comparison command returns `MIGRATION VERIFIED`.
- The reported trajectory divergence is `0.0` or within the published tolerance.

## What witnesses should not say

- Do not say a biological worm was uploaded.
- Do not say mind transfer was proven.
- Do not say life migrated.
- Do not say biological continuity was established.
- Do not say this has medical or human-transfer implications beyond the stated research ladder.
