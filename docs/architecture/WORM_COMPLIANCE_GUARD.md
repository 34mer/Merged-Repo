# Compliance Guard

This launch package is not an investment offering, not a securities document, and not legal, tax, accounting, medical, or investment advice.

Before accepting money, publish the approved legal structure for the capital pool. Options may include sponsored research agreements, grants, philanthropy, a nonprofit vehicle, a company financing, or another counsel-approved structure.

Do not promise returns, ownership, token value, priority medical access, biological immortality, therapeutic outcomes, human transfer, or proof of mind transfer.

Public language must preserve the claim boundary:

We verified lossless migration of a complete digital worm-like organism process between computational substrates.

## Required launch checks

- Legal structure reviewed before accepting money.
- Capital page reviewed before publication.
- Builder call reviewed for grant/procurement compliance.
- Public dashboard separates raised, committed, and contracted funds.
- All biological benchmark claims are framed as future research targets, not solved results.
