# Call for Builders: Biological C. elegans Transfer Benchmark

We are funding teams to build the next rung after verified digital substrate migration: individual-specific biological-to-digital or biological-to-synthetic transfer in C. elegans.

## Seeking

- C. elegans labs
- behavior recording teams
- microscopy and biological readout groups
- computational modelers
- simulation engineers
- robotics or synthetic-body teams
- validation and audit teams

## Benchmark target

Build an individual-specific C. elegans transfer benchmark spanning behavior recording, preservation/readout, individual-specific modeling, digital or synthetic re-embodiment, and validation.

## Required outputs

- biological protocol design
- dataset specification
- behavior fingerprinting standard
- readout feasibility report
- individual-specific model pipeline
- validation and audit protocol
- public milestone report

## Boundary

This call does not claim biological transfer is solved. It funds the benchmark needed to test the biological readout problem transparently.
